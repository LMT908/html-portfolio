My Top 3 movies So Far

All time favorite, really great movie about loving yourself and others for who they are inside. Ogres are like onions, they have layers!

Trolls

This kids movie has snuck it's way into my heart. I love the songs they include in all the Trolls movies and the overall message is always lovley.

The Proposal

My comfort movie. I love Ryan... that's it I just love him. His dynamic with Sandra is so good and of course Betty white is in it so it's automatically ICONIC!
